Half-dragon
-----------

Half-dragons are often more physically imposing than others of their kind that do not have dragon blood. Some show a hint of their heritage such as scaly skin or reptilian eyes.

### Half-dragon Traits

Half-dragon characters possess the following traits.

* +2 Charisma, &minus;2 Constitution.
* Medium: As Medium creatures, half-dragons have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
* Half-dragon base speed is 30 feet.
* Darkvision: Half-dragons can see in the dark up to 60 feet. Darkvision is black and white only, but it is otherwise like normal sight, and half-dragons can function just fine with no light at all.
* +1 natural armor bonus. Half-dragons have scaly hides.
* +2 racial bonus on Balance checks.
* +2 racial bonus to Knowledge (runes) and Forgery checks.

### Experimental

Breath weapon once per day.

Racial bonus to Balance, Jump, and Swim.

Favored class druid and sorcerer.

### References

This background is based on the SRD lizardfolk, half-dragon, and the mojh from _Arcana Unleashed_.
