Lizardfolk
----------

Lizardfolk favor the classes druid and sorcerer.

### Lizardfolk Traits

Lizardfolk characters possess the following traits.

* +2 Charisma, &minus;2 Constitution.
* Medium: As Medium creatures, lizardfolk have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
* Half-dragon base speed is 30 feet.
* Darkvision: Lizardfolk can see in the dark up to 60 feet. Darkvision is black and white only, but it is otherwise like normal sight, and lizardfolk can function just fine with no light at all.
* +1 natural armor bonus. Lizardfolk scales are remarkably tough.
* +2 racial bonus on Balance checks. Their tails help lizardfolk stay on their feet.

### Experimental

Reptilian subtype

Breath weapon once per day or hold breath ability.

* +2 racial bonus to Knowledge (runes) and Forgery checks.
