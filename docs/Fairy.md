Fairy
-----

Fairy characters possess the following racial traits.

__--4 Str, +4 Dex, +2 Int, +2 Cha__.

__Small size:__ +1 bonus to Armor Class, +1 bonus on attack rolls, +4 bonus on Hide checks, --4 penalty on grapple checks, lifting and carrying limits 3/4 those of Medium characters.

__Speed:__ A fairyâ€™s base land speed is 20 feet.

__Low-light vision__.

__Weapon Proficiency:__ Fairies are automatically proficient with the short sword and the composite longbow.

__Skills:__ Fairies have a +2 racial bonus on Listen, Search, and Spot checks.

__Spell-like abilities:__ 1/day---*dancing lights*, *detect magic*, *ghost sound*. Caster level 1st; save DC 10 + Cha modifier + spell level.

__Automatic Languages:__ Common, Sylvan, and a simple language that enables them to communicate on a very basic level with forest animals. Bonus Languages: Elven, Gnome, Halfling.

__Favored Class:__ Sorcerer.
