Spontaneous Casting
-------------------



Positive Energy
---------------

Some clerics are proficient at wielding positive energy. This allows them to spontaneously cast *cure* spells and turn undead.

Negative Energy
---------------

Some clerics can are proficient in a similar way with negative energy, allowing them to spontaneously cast *inflict* spells and rebuke undead.

Astral Energy
-------------

Some psionic manifestations work by manipulating astral energy.

Wild Energy
-----------

[The druid](ClassesI#druid) can use a form of divine energy to spontaneously cast *summon nature's ally* spells.
