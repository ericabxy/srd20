Maenad
------

<small>This material is Open Game Content, and is licensed for public use under the terms of the Open Game License v1.0a.</small>

Maenad characters possess the following racial traits.

__Medium:__ As Medium creatures, maenads have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.

__Speed:__ Maenad base land speed is 30 feet.

__Spell-like Ability:__ 1/day&mdash;*energy ray*. This spell-like ability works as *ray of frost* except it deals 1d6 of sonic damage. It is accompanied by a tremendous scream of rage. Caster level is equal to 1/2 Hit Dice (minimum 1st). The save DC is Cha-based.

__Automatic Languages:__ Common, Maenad. Bonus Languages: Aquan, Draconic, Dwarven, Elven, Goblin.

__Favored Class:__ Barbarian.
