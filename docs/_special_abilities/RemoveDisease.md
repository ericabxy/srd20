Remove Disease
--------------

As the [paladin](ClassesII#paladin) ability.

You can produce a [*remove disease*](SpellsP-R#remove-disease) effect, as the spell, once per week. You can take this feat multiple times, after each you can use this ability one additional time per week.

Notes
-----

Also gained by hospitaler.

Every three levels after 6th (twice per week at 9th, three times at 12th, and so forth).
