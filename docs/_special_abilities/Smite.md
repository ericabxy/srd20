Smite
-----

### Smite Evil

As the [paladin](ClassesII#paladin) ability.

#### Smite Evil 1/day

__Prerequisite:__ Base attack bonus +1.

__Benefit:__ Once per day, you may attempt to smite evil with one normal melee attack. You add your Charisma bonus (if any) to your attack roll and deal 1 extra point of damage per character level to a maximum of +4. If you accidentally smite a creature that is not evil, the smite has no effect, but the ability is still used up for that day.

#### Smite Evil 2/day

__Prerequisites:__ Base attack bonus +5.

__Benefit:__ Twice per day, extra damage maximum of +9.

#### Smite Evil 3/day

__Prerequisites:__ Base attack bonus +10.

__Benefit:__ Three times per day, extra damage maximum of +14.

#### Smite Evil 4/day

__Prerequisites:__ Base attack bonus +15.

__Benefit:__ Four times per day, extra damage maximum of +19.

#### Smite Evil 5/day

__Prerequisites:__ Base attack bonus +20.

__Benefit:__ Five times per day, extra damage maximum of +20.
