Favored Enemy
-------------

__Prerequisite:__ Base attack bonus +1.

__Benefits:__ 

__Special:__ This feat can be taken five times and bonuses stack.

Base attack bonus +1: 1st favored enemy.
Base attack bonus +5: 2nd favored enemy.
Base attack bonus +10: 3rd favored enemy.
Base attack bonus +15: 4th favored enemy.
Base attack bonus +20: 5th favored enemy.

Ranger: 1st, 5th, 10th, 15th, and 20th level.

Paladin Variant: as above, but limited to certain enemies, and lose lay on hands, turn undead, and remove disease.
