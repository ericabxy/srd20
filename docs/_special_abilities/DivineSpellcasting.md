Divine Spellcasting, 1st-level
------------------------------

You meditate or pray for your spells. You must choose a time at which you must spend 1 hour each day in quiet contemplation or supplication to regain your daily allotment of spells. Time spent resting has no effect on whether you can prepare spells. You may prepare and cast any spell you have access to, provided that you can cast spells of that level, but you must choose which spells to prepare during your daily meditation.

You gain access to the following spells: (0 level) 3/day---*create water, cure minor wounds, detect magic, detect poison, guidance, light, mending, purify food and drink, read magic, resistance, virtue*. (1st-level) 1/day---*cure light wounds, endure elements, magic stone, obscuring mist*.

You can choose to gain this feat one more time, at which point your 0-level and 1st-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day each.

Divine Spellcasting, 2nd-level
------------------------------

You gain access to the following spells: (2nd-level) 1/day---*Bear's endurance, bull's strength, delay poison, resist energy*.

You may gain this feat one more time, at which point your 0, 1st-, and 2nd-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day each.

Divine Spellcasting, 3rd-level
------------------------------

You gain access to the following spells: (3rd-level) 1/day---*Contagion, cure moderate wounds, daylight, meld into stone, protection from energy, remove disease, stone shape, water breathing, wind wall*.

You may gain this feat one more time, at which point your 2nd-level and 3rd-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day each.

Divine Spellcasting, 4th-level
------------------------------

You gain access to the following 4th-level spells: 1/day---**. In addition, your 0-level and 1st-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day each.

You may gain this feat one more time, at which point your 3rd-level and 4th-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day each.

Divine Spellcasting, 5th-level
------------------------------

You gain access to the following 5th-level spells: 1/day---**. In addition, your 2nd-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day.

You may gain this feat one more time, at which point your 4th-level and 5th-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day each.

Divine Spellcasting, 6th-level
------------------------------

You gain access to the following 6th-level spells: 1/day---**. In addition, your 1st-level and 3rd-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day each.

You may gain this feat one more time, at which point your 5th-level and 6th-level daily spell allotments increase by 1/day each.
