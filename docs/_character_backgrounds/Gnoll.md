Gnoll
-----

Gnolls are hyena-headed humanoids descended from a tribal society. Most gnolls have dirty yellow or reddish-brown fur.

* __Low-Light Vision.__ Gnolls can see twice as far as normal in starlight, moonlight, torchlight, and similar conditions of poor illumination. They retain the ability to distinguish color and detail under these conditions.
* +2 racial bonus on Gather Information, Handle Animal, Bluff, and Move Silently checks.
* __Bonus Feat:__ [Run](Feats#run-general).
* __Favored Class:__ Ranger.
