Esper
-----

The humans of Earth are slowly evolving the genetic ability to perform metaphysical feats. Contrary to the careful intelligence of Earth's metaphysicists, these humans exhibit the ability from charismatic force of self and can generally make magic happen at will.

### Esper Traits

* Medium-size: No special bonuses or penalties due to size.
* Base land speed is 30 feet.
* +2 genetic bonus on saving throws against enchantment spells or effects.
* +2 genetic bonus on Bluff, Diplomacy, and Sense Motive checks due to an esper's budding telepathy.
* Spell-Like Abilities: 1/day—*detect magic*, *mage hand*, *resistance*. Caster level 1st; save DC 10 + esper’s Cha modifier. Uses per day can be increased by taking the [Extra Uses Per Day](ExtraUsesPerDay) feat.
* Automatic Languages: Anglic. Bonus Languages: Any.
* Favored Class: Any.
