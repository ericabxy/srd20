Drakkin
-----------

Drakkins are humanoid creatures with scaly hides, elongated features, reptilian eyes, and exaggerated teeth and claws. 

Drakkins often favor the sorcerer class.

### Drakkin Traits

Drakkin characters possess the following traits.

* +2 Intelligence, --2 Constitution.
* Medium-size: As Medium creatures, drakkin base Armor Class is 10.
* Drakkin base land speed is 30 feet.
* Darkvision: Drakkin can see in the dark up to 60 feet. Darkvision is black and white only, but it is otherwise like normal sight, and drakkin can function just fine with no light at all.
* +1 natural armor bonus.
* +2 racial bonus on Balance checks thanks to their tails.

### Experimental

* Low-Light Vision: A drakkin can see twice as far as normal in starlight, moonlight, torchlight, and similar conditions of poor illumination. They retain the ability to distinguish color and detail under these conditions.

A drakkin has immunity to sleep and paralysis effects.

+2 racial bonus on Jump, Swim, and Balance checks thanks to a strong tail.

+2 racial bonus on Intimidate and Spot checks.

+2 Constitution, -2 Dexterity. Immunity to _sleep_ and paralysis effects. +2 racial bonus on Listen, Search, and Spot checks. Favored Class: Fighter.

+2 Charisma, -2 Constitution. Low-light vision.
