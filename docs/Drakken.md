Drakken
-------

Drakken are often more physically imposing than others of their kind that do not have dragon blood. Some show a hint of their heritage such as scaly skin or reptilian eyes.

Drakken characters possess the following racial traits.

__+2 Strength, +2 Constitution, +2 Charisma__.

__Medium:__ As Medium creatures, drakken have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.

__Darkvision__ out to 60 feet.

__Low-light vision__.

__+1 natural armor bonus__.

__Natural Weapons:__ 2 claws (1d4).

__+4 racial bonus__ on saves against magic sleep effects and paralysis.

__Favored Class:__ Fighter.

&mdash;Level adjustment +1.

### Sources

This background is based on the SRD half-dragon.
