## Glowglobe (Lesser)

- Evocation [Light]
- **Level:** 0 (Simple)
- **Casting Time:** Standard action
- **Range:** 0 feet
- **Effect:** Glowing sphere
- **Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
- **Saving Throw:** None
- **Spell Resistance:** No

This spell creates a 3-inch sphere that glows softly with the light of a torch, shedding illumination in a 20-foot radius from the point you touch. The effect is immobile. A *lesser glowglobe* does not function in an area of magical darkness.

The globe can be covered and hidden but not smothered or quenched.

**Heightened Effects:** The *lesser glowglobe* moves as the caster wills. The caster controls its movement as a free action. However, it never moves farther than 10 feet from the caster.
