---
title: Serpentipede
---

__Hit Die:__ d10.

__Skills:__ 4 + Int bonus.

__Base Attack Bonus:__ Equal to class level (as fighter).

__Good Saving Throws:__ Fortitude and Reflex.

__Weapon and Armor Proficiencies:__ Proficient was all natural weapons, but not proficient with any armor or shields.

__Spike Barrage (Ex):__ You may make a ranged attack using naturally-growing spikes stored within your carapace. Range Increment: 30 ft. Damage: 1d8 piercing. Counts as a manufactured ranged weapon for feats.

__Carapace:__ You gain a +2 natural armor bonus. This bonus increases by +1 at 4th level and every four levels thereafter.
