---
title: Olympian
---

--- Humanoid (human): Olympians are humanoid creatures with the human subtype.

--- Medium-size: No special bonuses or penalties due to size.

--- Base land speed is 30 ft.

__Endurance (Ex):__ Olympians gain a +4 bonus on the following checks and saves: Swim checks made to resist nonlethal damage, Constitution checks made to continue running, Constitution checks made to avoid nonlethal damage from a forced march, Constitution checks made to hold their breath, Constitution checks made to avoid nonlethal damage from starvation or thirst, Fortitude saves made to avoid nonlethal damage from hot or cold environments, and Fortitude saves made to resist damage from suffocation. Also, they may sleep in light or medium armor without becoming fatigued. 

__Run (Ex):__ When running, olympians move five times their normal speed (if wearing medium, light, or no armor and carrying no more than a medium load) or four times their speed (if wearing heavy armor or carrying a heavy load). When making a jump after a running start (see the Jump skill description), they gain a +4 bonus on that Jump check. While running, olympians retain their Dexterity bonus to AC.
