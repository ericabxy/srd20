Coastal Merrow Village
----------------------

A shallow cove with rocky outcroppings. Merrow live in huts made of coral and driftwood. The merrow here live by fishing the local waters and harvesting shellfish.

Hazards
-------

* Giant crabs have been attacking merrow farms.
* Tidal surge?
* Merrow ambush (if threatened, the merrow may attack with tridents and nets).
* Reef sharks patrol near the village.

Creatures
---------

* 3--6 merrow (or check stat block)
* 1--2 reef sharks or giant crabs

Rewards
-------

* Shells, pearls, crystals
* Local maps
* Potential alliance with merrows

Hooks
-----

* Broker a peace between the local locathah and merrow village.
* The village is threatened by migrating predators or aboleth.
* You are asked to recover a lost or stolen artifact before a merrow festival.
