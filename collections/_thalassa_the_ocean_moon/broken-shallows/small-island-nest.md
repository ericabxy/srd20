---
title: Small Island Nesting Ground
---

Location
--------

A rocky islet in the center of an ocean channel (strait?). Salt flats and tidal pools. Sparse vegetation. Its a nesting ground for giant crabs and large sea birds.

Hazards
-------

* Slippery rock faces
* Aggressive nesting animals
* Tidal changes cut of movement

Creatures
---------

* 2--4 giant crabs
* 1 large sea bird
* A single hiding chuul

Rewards
-------

* Feathers and shells for crafting
* Eggs for food or alchemy
* Driftwood and flotsam

Hooks
-----

* Capturing a particular creature for scientific research
* Animals roosting here have been attacking local routes on the mainland
* Retrieve an item or person before the tide returns
