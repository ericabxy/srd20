---
title: Shoreborn
---

--- Humanoid (human): Shoreborn are humanoid creatures with the human subtype.

--- Medium-size: No special bonuses or penalties due to size.

--- Base land speed is 30 ft.

--- 4 extra skill points at 1st level and 1 extra skill point at each additional level.

--- +2 cultural bonus on Climb, Swim, and Use Rope checks.

__Hold Breath (Ex):__ A shoreborn can hold its breath for a number of rounds equal to 6 x its Constitution score before it risks drowning.
