Taurus
------

Titan is home to a smaller species of minotaur.

Taurus often favor the barbarian or druid class.

### Taurus Traits

Taurus characters possess the following racial traits.

* +2 Str, +2 Dex, --4 Int (minimum 3), --2 Cha.
* Medium: As Medium creatures, taurus have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
* Taurus base land speed is 30 feet.
* Darkvision out to 60 feet.
* +2 racial bonus on Search, Spot, and Listen checks.
* Weapon Proficiency: Taurus are automatically proficient with the greataxe.
* Bull Horns: A taurus may learn the gore special ability.

### Sources

This background is based on the SRD [minotaur](/MonstersM-N#minotaurs-as-characters).
