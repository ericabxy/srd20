### Stonehauler

-  	Large Animal
- __Hit Dice:__ 5d8+15 (37 hp)
- __Initiative:__ +0
- __Speed:__ 40 ft. (8 squares)
- __Armor Class:__ 13 (–1 size, +4 natural), touch 9, flat-footed 13
- __Base Attack/Grapple:__ +3/+13
- __Attack:__ Gore +8 melee (1d8+9)
- __Full Attack:__ Gore +8 melee (1d8+9)
- __Space/Reach:__ 10 ft./5 ft.
- __Special Attacks:__ Stampede
- __Special Qualities:__ Low-light vision, scent
- __Saves:__ Fort +7, Ref +4, Will +1
- __Abilities:__ Str 22, Dex 10, Con 16, Int 2, Wis 11, Cha 4
- __Skills:__ Listen +7, Spot +5
- __Feats:__ Alertness, Endurance
- __Environment:__ Temperate plains
- __Organization:__ Solitary or herd (6–30)
- __Challenge Rating:__ 2
- __Advancement:__ 6–7 HD (Large)
- __Level Adjustment:__ —

These herd animals can be aggressive when protecting young and during the mating season, but they generally prefer flight to fighting. Haulers are broad-bodied, slow-moving animals with four hooves, blunt horns and powerful shoulders. They can stand more than 6 feet tall at the shoulder and are 9 to 12 feet long. It weighs 1,800 to 2,400 pounds. The hauler’s statistics can be used for almost any large herd animal.

Domesticated haulers are often decorated with talismans or paint. They are used for farming and hauling.

#### Combat

__Stampede (Ex):__ A frightened herd of haulers flee as a group in a random direction (but always away from the perceived source of danger). They literally run over anything of Large size or smaller that gets in their way, dealing 1d12 points of damage for each five haulers in the herd (Reflex DC 18 half ). The save DC is Strength-based.
