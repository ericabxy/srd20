Enhance Attribute
-----------------

__Prerequisites:__ Permanently give up the ability to [summon familiar](/SummonFamiliar).

__Benefit:__ Once per day, plus one additional time per five levels, you can add a +2 enhancement bonus to any on of your ability scores. This bonus lasts for a number of minutes equal to your level. Using this ability is a swift action.
