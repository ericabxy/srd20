---
title: Solari
---

Solari typically stand between 5 and 6 feet tall and weigh between 140 and 190 pounds, though individual variation is significant. No single body type predominates, and differences in build, stature, and proportions are common across populations. Men and women show broad overlap in height and mass.

--- Medium-size: No special bonuses or penalties due to size.

--- Base land speed is 30 feet.

--- 4 extra skill points at 1st level and 1 extra skill point at each additional level.

--- 1 extra bonus feat at 1st level.

Special Qualities
-----------------

Solari are widespread and adaptable, genetically shaped more by culture and circumstance than any single genetic trait. Their strengths come from flexibility, social fluency, and an ease with change.

Variance
--------

Solari exhibit a wide range of skin tones, hair colors and textures, and facial features, reflecting diverse origins and long-term habitation across varied environments. Their physiology follows standard humanoid norms, with proportions and movement suited to life under moderate gravity.
