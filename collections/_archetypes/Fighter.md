Fighter
-------

Base archetype with specializations.

Ranger Archetype
----------------

* Favored enemy
* Track
* Wild empathy
* Combat style
* Endurance
* Animal companion (coupled with divine magic?)
* Woodland stride
* Swift tracker
* Evasion
* Camouflage
* Hide in plain sight

### Terrain mastery track

Replaces favored terrain?

- Terrain mastery

### Hunt and pursuit track

Replaces favored enemy?

- Track
- Swift tracker
- Marking and pressure

### Survival and logistics track

The Aragorn aspect.

- Wilderness skills
- Camping, security, and foraging (Survival)
- Woodland stride and swift tracker accessed or shared
- Endurance accessed or shared
- Heal skill accessed or expanded

Design Rule
-----------

> If a class’s core fantasy is “martial competence plus context,” it belongs as an archetype of the martial base class, not as a separate class.
