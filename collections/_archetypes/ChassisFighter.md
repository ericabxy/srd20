Fighter Chassis
---------------

__Hit Die:__ d10.

__Good Saving Throw:__ Fortitude.

__Base Attack Bonus:__ Equal to level.

__Skills:__ 2 + Int modifier.

__Weapon and Armor Proficiency:__ The fighter chassis is proficient in the use of all simple and martial weapons and with light armor.

From this chassis you should be able to build a standard fighter (soldier background), a barbarian (barbaric background), a paladin (knight background), or a sort of thug/brute variant using options.

Soldier
-------

You gain proficiency with medium and heavy armor and with shields. You gain a fighter bonus feat at 1st level, 2nd level, and every two levels thereafter (4th, 6th, 8th, and so on).

__Bonus Feats:__ As fighter.

Barbaric
--------

Your Hit Die increases to d12 and you gain proficiency with medium armor, [fast movement](/FastMovement), and the ability to [rage](/Rage) once per day. At 4th level and every four levels thereafter you gain the ability to rage one more time per day.

At 7th level you gain [damage reduction](/DamageReduction) 1/&mdash;. Every three levels thereafter you increase your damage reduction by +1/&mdash;. Gain special abilities from the following list: uncanny dodge, trap sense +1 (stackable), improved uncanny dodge, greater rage, indomitable will, tireless rage, mighty rage.

Knightly
--------

You must be of lawful good alignment.

You gain proficiency with medium and heavy armor and with shields. You also gain [aura of good](/Aura) and can use [detect evil](/DetectEvil) at will.

You gain [smite evil](/Smite) 1/day. Every fifth level you increase smite evil +1/day.
