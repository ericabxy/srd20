---
title: Marine
---

__Alignment:__ Any lawful.

__Hit Die:__ d10.

__Skills:__ 2 + Int.

__Base Attack Bonus:__ Equal to class level (as fighter).

__Good Saving Throw:__ Fortitude.

__Weapon and Armor Proficiencies:__ All simple and martial weapons, all armor and shields (including tower shields).

__Marine Weapon:__ You gain weapon focus with one ranged weapon of your choice. That weapon counts as a "marine weapon" for all class features. You may draw or reload a marine weapon as a free action.

__Armor Interface:__ At 2nd level, while wearing medium or heavy armor, reduce armor check penalties by 2 (minimum 0) and increase maximum Dex bonus by +1

__Stimpack:__ At 3rd level, you may activate a stimpack as a _____ action. __Stimpack Effect:__ +2 to Strength and Dexterity, +10 ft. enhancement bonus to speed, and +1 extra attack at your highest base attack bonus when making a full attack. __Duration:__ 3 rounds. After the effect ends, you take 2d6 damage and are fatigued for 1 minute. __Uses Per Day:__ 1 + Con modifier (minimum 1).

__Marine Weapon Specialization:__ You gain weapon specialization as a bonus feat with your marine weapon, even if you do not meet the prerequisites.
