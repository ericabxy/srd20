Trogle
---------

### Trogle Traits

---Humanoid (human): Trogle are humanoids with the human subtype.

---Medium-size: No bonuses or penalties due to size.

---Trogle base land speed is 30 feet.

---Darkvision: Trogle can see in the dark up to 60 feet. Darkvision is black and white only, but it is otherwise like normal sight, and Trogle can function just fine with no light at all.

---4 extra skill points at 1st level and 1 extra skill point at each additional level.

--- +4 natural bonus on Hide checks. In rocky or underground settings, this bonus improves to +8.

--- +2 natural bonus on Listen checks.
