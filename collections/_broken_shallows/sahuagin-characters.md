Sahuagin
--------

### Sahuagin Traits

--- +2 Strength, --2 Intelligence.

--- Medium-size monstrous humanoid (aquatic).

--- Locathah base land speed is 20 feet, and swim speed is 30 feet.

--- +4 natural bonus on Handle Animal checks when working with sharks.

__Amphibious (Ex):__ Sahuagin can breathe both air and water.

__Darkvision (Ex):__ Sahuagin can see in the dark up to 60 feet. Darkvision is black and white only, but it is otherwise like normal sight, and sahuagin can function just fine with no light at all.

__Powerful Jaws:__ Sahuagin may gain the [bite](/Bite) ability in place of a normal feat or a bonus feat.

Adventure Hooks
---------------

Hunting raids, tribal rivalries, moral dilemmas, interspecies conflict.

### Deep Sahuagin

This saltwater variant of the sahuagin species is adapted to the environment of the deep sea.
