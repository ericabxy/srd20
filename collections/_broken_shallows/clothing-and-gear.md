title: Clothing and Gear
---

- Studded reef sandals
- Wrapped or bare calves
- Light breeches tied at the knee
- A breathable wrap-shirt or vest
- A short open coat or working mantle
- A hooded waterproof mantle for weather
- Goggles or shaded lenses by day

__Traveler's Outfit:__ Studded sandals for protection and traction, feet and calves either bare or wrapped with a breathable weave. Tight breeches cinched mid-calf, open tunic that can close in the front. Waterproof mantle with low hood. Shaded or tinted goggles to resist glare.

### Reef Sandals

The dominant footwear on Thalassa tends toward _open sandals_ with thick, waterproof soles and solid straps. Many Thalassans purposefully tan and callous their feet but protection against sharp rocks and rough coral are paramount.
