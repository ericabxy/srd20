---
title: About this module
---

_The Broken Shallows_ is a short adventure module designed to introduce the world of Thalassa, an ocean moon in orbit around the gas giant Iovis.

Reading the encounter entries
-----------------------------

Encounters sometimes contain a creature entry with statistics listed.

__Creature Name (number encountered):__ Type (subtype); Hit Dice (hit points); Inititative bonus; Speed; Armor Class; Attack Bonus/Grapple; Attack; Full Attack; Special Abilities; Special Qualities; Saves; Ability Scores.
