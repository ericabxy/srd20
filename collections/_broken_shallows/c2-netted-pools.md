---
title: Locathah Tribe
---

Here a tribe of locathah live in a cluster of villages in the shallows. Living and workplace structures are built above and below the waterline, connected by rope bridges, netting, and coral tunnels.

__Population:__ Almost 200 locathah live in and around the shallows.

__Tribe Commoner (90--100):__ Locathah; Medium-size humanoid (aquatic); HD 2d8 (9 hp); Init +1; Spd 10 ft., swim 60 ft.; AC 14 (+1 Dex, +3 natural);

__Tribe Sergeant (9--10):__ Locathah War3; Medium-size humanoid (aquatic); HD 3d8 (13 hp);

__Tribe Lieutenant (5):__ Locathah War5; Medium-size humanoid (aquatic); HD 5d8 (22 hp);

__Tribe Captain (3):__ Locathah War7; Medium-size humanoid (aquatic); HD 7d8 (31 hp);
