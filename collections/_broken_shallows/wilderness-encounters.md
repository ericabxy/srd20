Wilderness Encounters
---------------------

Consult [wilderness and environment](/WildernessandEnvironment) rules and adapt to the following list.

1. Rogue Tide (Ocean or Coastal): Sudden tidal surge, Swim or Reflex save or be swept into pools.
2. Giant Crab (Island or Shore): 1--2 aggressive, nesting grabs.
3. Seabird Flock (Ocean or Island): Roosting or migrating, minor hazard, opportunity to harvest feathers/eggs.
4. Reef Shark (Coast or Shallows): 1--3 sharks attack swimmers/fishers.
5. Bioluminescent Fauna Swarm (Deep Ocean): Glowing fish or cephalopods passing.
6. Locathah Scout (Coastal or Shore): 1--2 locathah warriors may observe, trade, or warn of nearby predators.
7. Rogue Giant Lobster (Shallow Ocean or Island Pool): 1--2 ambush predators.
8. Ice-Floe or Slush Drift (Ocean or Open Water): Floating ice or salt crust. Pilot, Balance, or Climb check.
9. Deep Trench Currents (Ocean): Strong underwater currents, Pilot or Swim check to avoid being swept off course.
10. Merrow Patrol (Coastal): 2--4 merrow warriors. Hostile or curious.
11. Aboleth Sensing (Deep Ocean or Waters): Party can sense psionic disturbance from a lairing or moving aboleth. Minor effects or challenge.
12. Shipwreck or Flotsam (Coastal or Ocean): Debris from other travelers, may contain treasure or hazard.
13. Salt Storm (Island or Coastal): Abrasive winds, reduce movement and Fortitude save for exhaustion.
14. Fungal Bloom (Island Valley): Bioluminescent, slippery, Acrobatics or Climb check.
15. Nomadic Sea Sprite (Ocean): May help or hinder travelers.
16. Tide-pool Predator (Shallow or Coastal Ocean): 1--3 sea snakes or scorpionfish 1--3.
17. Migratory Kraken Juvenile (Ocean): Usually only 1.
18. Salt Crust Collapse (Coastal or Island): Reflex save or fall into shallow pools.
19. Floating Ice Coral (Ocean): Can be harvested. Swim check.
20. Weather Phenomenon (Any): storm, fog, or lightshow may impact navigation.
