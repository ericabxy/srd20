The mere is a hominid species with aquatic adaptations. They are not water-breathers nor amphibious, but larger oxygen reserves and aquadynamic limbs allow them to move faster and stay longer underwater.

Mere Traits
-----------

---Humanoid (human): Mere are humanoid creatures with the human subtype.

---Medium-size: No special bonuses or penalties due to size.

---Mere base land speed is 30 feet

---Swim speed 15 feet: A mere can move through water at its swim speed without making Swim checks. It has a +8 racial bonus on any Swim check to perform some action or avoid a hazard. It can always choose to take 10 on a Swim check, even if distracted or endangered. It can use the run action while swimming, provided it swims in a straight line.

---Low-light Vision: Mere can see twice as far as normal in starlight, moonlight, and similar conditions of poor illumination. They retain the ability to distinguish color and detail under these conditions.
