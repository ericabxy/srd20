---
title: The Circumsolar Regions
---

The Circumsolar Regions are the area of space around Sol containing all objects gravitationally bound to the star, including the inner and outer Solar System, the Kuiper belt, and the Oort cloud.

The Solar System proper is inhabited by myriad species of humanoid and non-humanoid creatures. Some of the most prevalent during the Late Interstitial Age are as follows:

_[Blue](Blue):_ A goblinoid primate species that migrated from the outer regions over the last millenia.

_[Dromite](Dromite):_ An insectoid species adapted to low gravity, thin atmosphere, and darkness.

_[Duren](Duergar):_ A short-statured humanoid evolved to withstand environmental stresses.

_[Elan](Elan):_ A humanoid species with an uncannily ethereal presence.

_[Eldren](Half-giant):_ A large humanoid race believed by some to be descended from the creators of civilization.

_[Maenad](Maenad):_ Tall humanoids known for controlled outbursts of emotional and physical energy.

_[Solari](Solari):_ Versatile humanoids known to adapt to a wide variety of environments and lifestyles.

_[Xeph](Xeph):_ A lean and agile humanoid race known for dexterity and quick movements.
