Reach Circulator
================

Maintains material and informational circulation between settlements. Moves perishables, surplus, warnings, news, and communications. They deliver surplus consumables before it can spoil, exchange excess and shortage between communities, deliver formal obligations (I.O.U.s, etc.), redistributes tools and equipment to where they are needed. Often responsible for several settlements in one trip.

Circulators are recognized by face or mode of dress, and practically guaranteed shelter on arrival and for extended stays. They are given escorts if they must leave through dangerous roads. Before leaving any settlement, they gather unneeded and surplus materials, news and information, and prepare to deliver them to their next destination.

__Reach Circulator:__ 3rd-level Expert;

Str 9, Dex 8, Con 10, Int 12, Wis 13, Cha 11

_Skills and Feats:_ Profession (supply logistics) +7, Sense Motive +7, Diplomacy +6, Survival +7, Knowledge (local) +7, Gather Information +6; Endurance.
