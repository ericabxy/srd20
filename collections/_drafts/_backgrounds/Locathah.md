Locathah
--------

* +2 Dexterity, +2 Intelligence, +2 Wisdom.
* Aquatic Subtype. A locathah can breathe underwater. It cannot also breathe air. A locathah can hold its breath outside the water for 2 rounds per point of Constitution. After that, it begins to suffocate.
* Locathah base land speed is 10 ft.
* Swim Speed 60 ft. A locathah can move through water at this speed without making Swim checks. It has a +8 racial bonus on any Swim check to perform some action or avoid a hazard. It can always choose to take 10 on a Swim check, even if distracted or endangered. It can use the run action while swimming, provided it swims in a straight line.
* Level Adjustment: +1
