Deep Ocean (Abyssal Creatures)
------------------------------

### Aboleth

Apex species of the deepest oceans. Highly intelligent with psychic/spell-like abilities. Live in trenches, guard dungeons.

### Kraken

Enormous cephalopod predators. Encountered mostly as young who hunt in shallower water than adults. Attack ships and sometimes coast-dwellers. Tentacles are bioluminescent.

### Chuul

Underwater cave-dwellers. Found near continental shelves and hydrothermal vents. Glowing sensory organs.

### Giant Octopod

Natural predator of the deep. Use [giant octopus](/MonstersAnimals#octopus-giant). Some squirt bioluminescent ink.

### Sea Hag

Encountered in regular and deep sea variety. See [sea hag](/MonstersH-I#sea-hag). Preys on unwary travelers. Deep sea appearence: frond-like hair, algae growths on skin.

Coastal (Intertidal Creatures)
------------------------------

### Locathah

Live in shallows around island chains, coastal cities. Mostly neutral or friendly with land-dwellers. See [locathah](/MonstersK-L#locathah). Skilled fishers and traders.

### Merrow

Amphibious, live near and sometimes raid shorelines. Sometimes hostile, sometimes neutral. See [merrow](/MonstersO-R#merrow). Appearence: dark skin with coral nodules.

### Shambling Mound

Animate seaweed or algae that grows along reefs or water farms. Territorial hazard. See [shambling mound](/MonstersS#shambling-mound).

### Sea Lion or Giant Otter

Guard fishing grounds

### Reef Shark

Lurk in shallow waters near reefs, islands.

### Merfolk

A more reclusive and mysterious community compared to the merrow and locathah.

### Giant Serpent

Ocean predators. Consider adapting some [dragons](/MonstersDr-Dw#dragon) to this role.

Island (Shoreline Denizens)
---------------------------

### Giant Crab

Encountered alone or in swarms. Sometimes predate coastal livestock, eggs.

### Large Sea Bird (Large Roc Variant)

Flying predator and biggest avian on Thalassa. Gull or albatross analog. See [roc](/MonstersO-R#roc).

### Sea Sprite (Water Weird)

Rare guardian spirits of small coves, springs. See [sprite](/MonstersO-R#sprite). May help or hinder travelers.

### Crocodile

Shallows-dwelling ambush predator.

### Giant Lizard

Island predators. Consider adapting some [dragons](/MonstersDr-Dw#dragon) to this role.

Roles
-----

Aboleths guard high-level dungeons.

Locathah inhabit coastal societies.

Kraken juveniles or giant octopii are seasonal, migratory repeating threats.

Sharks, merrow, and sea hags may harry travelers between islands.
