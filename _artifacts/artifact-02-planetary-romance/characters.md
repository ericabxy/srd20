## Mikael the Cleric

Mikael is the half-giant lord of a keep housing a space elevator to a geostationary space-port. During his life he's served at various times as consul, diplomat, and judge for the local canal-citadel.

__Background:__ Half-giant channeler.

__Abilities:__ Great Wisdom.
