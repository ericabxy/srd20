# Characters

## Mikael --- Half-Giant Cleric of Skyreach Hold

Mikael is the half-giant lord of a keep housing a space elevator to a geostationary space-port. During his life he's served at various times as consul, diplomat, and judge for the local canal-citadel.

__Type:__ Medium-size giant.

__Ability Scores:__ Str 13, Dex 8, Con 14, Int 10, Wis 16, Cha 12.

__Special Qualities:__ Powerful build.

_Typical Divine Spells Prepared_ (5/3/2; save DC 13 + spell level): 0---*detect magic, light, guidance, resistance, virtue;* 1st---*bless, divine favor, shield of faith;* 2nd---*bull's strength, lesser restoration.*

## Haller-Thane --- Duergar Fighter and Engineer

Haller is a duergar miner, warrior, and engineer.

__Type:__ Medium-size humanoid (duergar).

__Ability Scores:__ Str 16, Dex 13, Con 14, Int 10, Wis 12, Cha 8.

__Feats:__ Weapon focus, power attack.

__Bonus Feats (fighter):__ (3), improved initiative, weapon specialization.

## Gessica --- Rigelian Blue Contract Pilot of _The Long Spiral_

Gessica is a Rigelian blue, a goblin-like simian humanoid from the Kuiper belt. She pilots a small freighter and operates several space-lanes and ports for transit of personnel and cargo.

__Size/Type:__ Small humanoid (blue).

__Ability Scores:__ Str 10, Dex 16, Con 13, Int 14, Wis 10, Cha 8.

__Speed:__ 30 ft.

__Hit Die:__ d6.

__Skills:__ Knowledge (local) +9, Navigate +9, Pilot +13, Profession (transporter) +7.

__Feats:__ Aircraft operation (spacecraft), skill focus (pilot).

## Alienor --- Elan Psionic Mystic and Natural Philosopher

__Speed:__ 30 ft.
